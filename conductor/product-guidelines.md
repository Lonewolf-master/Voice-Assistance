# Product Guidelines: Jarvis\n\n## Tone and Style\n- **Professional & Concise:** Jarvis should provide clear, direct, and efficient responses, minimizing unnecessary filler.\n- **Sophisticated Companion:** The assistant should maintain a persona that is both professional and friendly, referring to the user with a respectful title (e.g., "Sir" or "Ma'am") while building a helpful rapport.\n\n## Visual Identity (CLI)\n- **State Indicators:** Use clear text labels or emojis (e.g., 🎙️ Listening, 🧠 Thinking, 🔊 Speaking) to provide immediate feedback on Jarvis's current status.\n- **Detailed Logging:** Provide timestamped event logs to help developers and power users monitor system processes and background tasks.\n\n## Voice and Speech (TTS)\n- **Classic Sophistication:** Jarvis's voice should feature a crisp, British accent to align with the traditional persona, prioritizing clarity and natural intonation.\n\n## Speech Recognition (STT)\n- **Real-time Feedback:** Transcriptions should be displayed in the CLI as the user speaks to provide immediate visual confirmation.\n- **Contextual Accuracy:** The system should leverage conversation history to improve transcription accuracy over time.
